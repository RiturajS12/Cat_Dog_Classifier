Fun Facts About Cats and Dogs

  • Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four on their back paws.
  • Dogs' noses are unique, much like human fingerprints.
  • Cats can jump up to six times their length.
  • Dogs can learn more than 1000 words.
  • Cats purr at a frequency that promotes healing.
  • Dogs can smell your feelings! They can pick up on subtle changes in your scent that reflect how you're feeling.
  • Whisker fatigue is a real thing! Cats can become overwhelmed by too much stimulation of their whiskers.
  • Dogs' sense of smell is 40 times greater than humans. They can even detect certain diseases like cancer.
  • Cats sleep for 70% of their lives. They need all that sleep to recharge for their bursts of energy.
